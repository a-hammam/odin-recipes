Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken in a spiced curry sauce. It's perfect with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cayenne pepper, garlic, and ginger. Add the chicken and mix until coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the chicken onto skewers and grill until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove from skewers and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and continue to simmer until the sauce thickens, about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the grilled chicken to the sauce and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala with rice or naan bread.
