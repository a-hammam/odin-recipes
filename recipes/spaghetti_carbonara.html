Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 4 ounces pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
